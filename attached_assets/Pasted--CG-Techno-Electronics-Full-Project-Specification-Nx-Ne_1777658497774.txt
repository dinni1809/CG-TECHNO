# CG Techno Electronics — Full Project Specification (Nx + Next.js + Animations)

---

## 0. OBJECTIVE

Build a **premium, modern, animated corporate website** for:

**CG Techno Electronics (Bengaluru)**

The website must:

* Look like a **high-end IT company**
* Be **interactive and animated (NOT static)**
* Showcase services, products, and clients
* Capture enquiries & job applications
* Send all submissions to:
  **[cgtechnoelectronics@gmail.com](mailto:cgtechnoelectronics@gmail.com)**
* Be fast, responsive, and deployable on Vercel

---

## 4. ANIMATION SYSTEM (VERY IMPORTANT)

The website MUST NOT feel static.

Use **Framer Motion everywhere strategically**.

---

### GLOBAL RULES

* All sections animate on scroll (fade + slide up)
* Duration: 0.6–0.8s
* Use `whileInView`
* Use `viewport={{ once: true }}`

---

### HERO SECTION

* Text fades in + slides up
* Buttons scale on hover
* Background gradient animation (slow)

---

### SERVICES / PRODUCT CARDS

* Hover:

  * lift (translateY)
  * shadow increase
  * slight scale (1.02–1.05)

---

### NAVBAR

* Sticky on scroll
* Background blur (`backdrop-blur-md`)
* Smooth transition

---

### SCROLL REVEAL

Example behavior:

* Section appears when scrolled into view
* Fade + upward motion

---

### STATS SECTION

* Animated counters (count from 0)
* Smooth increment effect

---

### BUTTONS

* Hover scale
* Color transition
* Optional glow

---

### PAGE TRANSITIONS

* Fade transition between pages
* No abrupt changes

---

### MICRO INTERACTIONS

* Links underline on hover
* Cards slightly tilt or move
* Smooth easing
